A vanilla berry cake

A vanilla berry cake is a light, moist dessert featuring vanilla-flavored sponge layers, often filled and topped with a mix of fresh berries (strawberries, raspberries, blueberries, blackberries) and a creamy frosting, such as whipped cream, cream cheese, or mascarpone. It is popular for celebrations like birthdays, anniversaries, and summer gatherings. strawberry cake

Strawberry cake is a dense, moist, and often vibrant pink dessert featuring fresh, frozen, or freeze-dried strawberries, or strawberry gelatin. Originating in the mid-19th century, it is commonly served with strawberry buttercream or whipped cream, making it a popular choice for spring celebrations. The cake typically uses cream cheese, butter, and cake flour for a tender, fine crumb

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch cake pan.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla.
  3. Mix flour and baking powder. Alternate adding dry ingredients and milk to the wet mixture.
  4. Fold in berries gently. Pour into pan and bake for 40-45 minutes.
  5. Cool and serve. Enjoy your berry cake!
